# Odie's Epic

1\. Odie O'Dool hath been a fool

2\. Soaked in drool he dreamt of jewels

3\. There he lay, the tattered tot

4\. Beat and battered on his cot

5\. Thinking thoughts, 

6\. His mind had lots,

7\. Connecting dots others could not.
